# Business Impact

This project is not just an LLM demo or a chat bot; it acts like an **autonomous workflow layer** sitting on top of existing HR, IT, procurement, and ticketing systems. It improves three things that matter in real enterprises: **speed**, **accuracy**, and **auditability**.

---

## 1. Employee onboarding

- Today, HR + IT spend roughly 15–30 min per onboarding stitching forms, emails, and tickets

### How it works in real orgs (today)
- If it saves even 10 minutes per hire.

### Potential business impact
- 10 min × 30 = 300 min ≈ 5 hours/month of HR/IT time freed.

---

## 2. Meeting action extraction & clarification

- Every employee is attending atleast 3-5 meetings per day. Each meeting has 3–5 action items, but only 60–70% are captured into any system today.

### How it works in real orgs (today)
- If you lift capture from 65% → 90%, and each month there are 15-25 meetings × 4 actions = ~(60 to 100) actions.

### Potential business impact

- If even half of those extra actions represent 1 hour of work each, that is 20-50 hours of work saved per month.

---

## 3. Procurement & SLA risk handling

- A team processes 100 procurement requests/month.
- Each stuck item needs 30–60 min of manual chasing and coordination.

### How it works in real orgs (today)

- - 10–15% (10–15 items) tend to get stuck due to unresponsive approvers or vacations.

### Potential business impact

- If it automatically catches and reroutes even 10 stuck requests/month, and saves 30 min of manual chasing per request:
- 10 × 0.5 hours = 5 hours/month of ops/manager time saved.

---

### Bottom Words

- In this generation, time directly converts to money.
- Even at a conservative blended cost of 1,000 INR/hour, these workflows can unlock 30–60K INR/month in productivity for a small team—without adding headcount.
